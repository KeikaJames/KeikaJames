### 🛠 How I Work

I keep my public presence intentionally minimal. Visibility is easy to measure; rigor is not.  
In practice, narrative confidence tends to scale faster than epistemic discipline—and I am generally more interested in the latter.

Some of this work is written in places not especially optimized for productivity: ski resort hotels, hot coffee nearby, fewer reasons to rush. ⛷️☕️  
This has not improved the code, but it has occasionally improved the decisions.

---

### 📦 About This Space

Most repositories here are exploratory or infrastructural.  
They exist to test assumptions, surface failure modes, and occasionally confirm that stopping early was the right choice.

If something looks unfinished, it probably is—and probably on purpose.
